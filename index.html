Shaking Across Scales: Global Earthquake Trends and Regional Surveillance Gaps

On a planet of constant motions, hundreds of earthquakes ripple through across continents and fault lines. Although some regions rumble under a dense web of seismic sensors while others shake almost unnoticed, most never make headlines. How do we prevent such quakes from slipping through the cracks of our monitoring networks? We uncover the gaps in monitoring as we explore regional seismic coverage and map global earthquakes.
